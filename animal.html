ANIMALS MAP PLACES

ELEPHANTS

The elephant is the largest land animal on Earth.

GEMSBOKS

The gemsbok is a large antelope with long, straight horns.

GIRAFFES

The giraffe is the tallest land animal on Earth.

GORILLAS

The gorilla is the largest primate on Earth.

RHINO BEETLES

Rhino beetles are known for their impressive horns and strength.

KOALAS

Koalas are marsupials that are native to Australia.

LIONS

The lion, often referred to as the king of the jungle, lives in social groups called prides.

MONKEYS

Monkeys are highly social animals that live in troops.

PANDAS

Pandas are large bears that are native to China.

TIGERS

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world.

WARTHOGS

Warthogs are wild pigs that are native to Africa.
